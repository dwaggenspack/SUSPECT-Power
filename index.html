Plants within search area:

Do you SUSPECT there may be a power plant near you? Does it THRILL you?

Does it instill a crippling TERROR within you? Either way, this map has you covered!

There are 2 ways of performing this magical query to solve your quandry:

  1. Click on the map.
  2. Search for an address by clicking the magnifying glass on the map.

You may change the buffer distance at any time with the slider at the bottom of the map

You may also toggle on and off any fuel types you don't care for using the labelled checkboxes on the right side of the map.
